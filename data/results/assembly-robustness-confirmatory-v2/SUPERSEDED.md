# Superseded Evidence

This evidence version is retained for audit history but must not be used for publication claims.

Reason: scenario identifiers rounded backlash values to three decimal places. The distinct 0.0005 mm and 0.001 mm conditions were both stored as `backlash-0.001` and merged during aggregation. The semantic evidence verifier introduced after the final AEI review council rejects this bundle.

Required replacement: a new immutable protocol and result version with injective scenario identities, statistically declared estimands and contrasts, and raw-to-summary semantic verification.
