# AEI Remediation Status

## Decision Scope

This register maps every finding from `reviews/aei-final-council/evidence-ledger.md` to current authoritative evidence. A finding is `resolved` only when the original invalid claim is removed or the requested evidence exists and is verified. `Partial` and `external` items remain limitations or submission blockers.

The defensible contribution is a fully digital, object-oriented engineering-informatics workflow for a bounded compound-spur family. It demonstrates centralized executable semantics, solver-hidden briefs, independently checked positive certificates, replayable bounded negative proofs, and preregistered finite-census assembly inference. It does not demonstrate structural qualification, manufacturing yield, industrial effectiveness, topology-general performance, or physical validation.

## Current Computed Results

- Knowledge ablation: all representations answer unmutated competency cases; typed and duplicated-semantic encodings detect and localize all mutations, while typed semantics use one rule site versus seven.
- Replayable proof study: 40 negative cases, 262,440 hashed tuple dispositions, 6,063 evaluated placements, zero engineering tolerance, complete semantic replay.
- Confirmatory assembly study: 7,864,320 draws; interaction estimate `-0.022440`, adjusted interval `[-0.022578, -0.022301]`; global housing equivalence rejected.
- Outer-replicated selection: mean robust-minus-nominal modeled acceptance `0.003206`, interval `[0.003051, 0.003362]` across 12 train-select-test repetitions; preregistered `0.005` claim not supported.
- Planetary external formulation: 3 of 12 fixed-seed runs meet the threshold, exact interval `[0.055, 0.572]`, and best objective `0.525588660`; conversion remains pending independent review.
- Structural model: refinement gate failed; no strength admission, redesign, feasibility, safety, or qualification claim remains.

## Submission Conditions

The repository-owned computational submission is complete only after the v4 paper bundle, v3 provenance aggregate, fresh v3 clean attestation, and a new independent review council find no critical contradiction. Actual journal submission additionally requires:

1. Independent approval or removal of the planetary conversion claim.
2. Author names, affiliations, corresponding-author details, funding, and competing-interest declarations.
3. An author-selected license plus citation metadata.
4. A public archival release and persistent DOI.

An independent-user impact study and systematic literature protocol would materially strengthen AEI prospects but cannot be truthfully self-generated as external evidence.
